Our Services

Introducing our comprehensive range of Professional cleaning services at Hydra Clean. We take pride in delivering top-tier cleaning solutions that are tailored to meet your specific needs. Whether it's residential or commercial cleaning, carpet and upholstery cleaning, tile and grout cleaning, or pet odor removal, our experienced team is committed to ensuring your space is not just clean, but also healthy and inviting. Let us take care of the cleaning, so you can enjoy the benefits of a pristine and fresh environment.

Carpet Cleaning

Our professional carpet cleaning services are designed to revitalize your carpets, removing deep-seated dirt, stains, and allergens to leave your space looking and feeling fresh. Our experienced technicians use cutting-edge equipment and environmentally friendly solutions to ensure thorough cleaning, stain removal, odor elimination, and allergen control. With quick drying times and flexible scheduling options, we make the process convenient for you, offering not just clean carpets but also a healthier living or working environment. Choose us for a transformation that preserves the beauty and longevity of your carpets while enhancing the overall cleanliness of your space.

Upholstery Cleaning

Our professional upholstery cleaning services are dedicated to renewing the appearance and comfort of your furniture. Over time, upholstery can accumulate dirt, stains, and allergens, affecting the aesthetics and hygiene of your home or office. We specialize in deep cleaning all types of upholstery, from sofas and chairs to drapes and curtains.

Tile & Grout Cleaning

Our tile and grout cleaning services are your solution for restoring the beauty and cleanliness of your tiled surfaces. Over time, grime, dirt, and moisture can accumulate, leading to discolored and grimy grout lines and dull-looking tiles. We specialize in revitalizing a wide range of tiled areas, from kitchen floors and bathroom walls to entryway tiles and outdoor patios.

Pet Odor Removal

Our pet odor removal services are designed to help you reclaim a fresh and odor-free living environment, even in homes with furry family members. While we adore our pets, they can sometimes leave behind unwanted odors that are challenging to eliminate with ordinary cleaning products. That's where we come in.
